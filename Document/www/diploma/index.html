About the Program

IIT Madras is offering Diplomas in Programming and Data Science for college students, working professionals and job seekers who aim to build a career in these domains. The convenience of online learning combined with in-person assessments makes the program flexible and affordable while retaining the exceptional quality of learning that IIT Madras stands for.
